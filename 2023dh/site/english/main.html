Tour Attractions

Mangsang Auto Camping Resort Mangsang Auto Camping Resort is a four-season family resort consisting of a variety of facilities including a resort area with a unique skyline embodying white waves and black rocks, a beach hanok area full of elegant hanoks situated next to the beautiful seashore, and an auto campground with facilities preserving the natural view intact. More View

Mureung gyegok Valley

The Mureunggyegok Valley known as a paradise where gods live in is a valley located between Dutasan Mountain and Cheongoksan Mountain. When you see a beautiful view of numerous strange rock formations and cliffs, you will feel like you are in a paradise. More View

ChuamChotdaebawi Rock

Chuam Chotdaebawi Rock harmonizing with other nearby strange rock formations appears at the beginning of the national anthem (Aegukga) video. The sunrise from behind Chuam Chotdaebawi Rock is very beautiful and popular with tourists. More View

Cheongok Golden Bat Cave

Cheongok Golden Bat Cave in Donghae is a horizontal limestone cave stretching for 1,510 meters (only 810 meters open to the public). Estimated to have been created 400 to 500 million years ago, it is Korea’s only cave located in a downtown area. More View

Dojjaebigol Sky Valley

Dojjaebigol Sky Valley, located between Mukho Lighthouse and Wolsotaekji, provides a variety of observatory facilities and attractions so tourists can enjoy a beautiful view of the East Sea. More View
